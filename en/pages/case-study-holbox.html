About Client

Holbox is a leader in creative and innovative displays and POS (Point of Sale) materials. The company specializes in designing, creating, and producing advertising stands, with a strong focus on sustainability and ecology.

Challenge

At the end of the month, employees had to manually create a report with all orders, transcribing data from hundreds of emails to Excel. It was a tedious, time-consuming process prone to mistakes.

  • Huge time loss

    30-40 hours per month spent on manual work.

  • Risk of errors

    Manually transcribing data from hundreds of orders generated mistakes.

Solution

We implemented an automation monitoring the mailbox, which detects new orders, analyzes their content, and automatically enters data into the report file.

1. Email Monitoring

The system automatically checks the inbox for new emails with orders.

2. Intelligent Analysis

The algorithm extracts key data from the email content based on defined rules.

3. Automatic Entry

Data is immediately added to the Excel/Sheets report, ready for further processing.

Results

Savings and Precision
