Challenge

Lack of a central fleet management system led to information chaos and financial risk.

  • Decentralization

    Difficulties in tracking vehicle location and status between branches.

  • Insurance Gaps

    Risk of missing insurance and inspection deadlines, threatening high penalties.

Solution

We created a dedicated system centralizing fleet management with automatic notifications.

1. Central Database

One system collecting all vehicle information.

2. Automatic Reminders

The system monitors insurance and inspection dates, sending advance notifications.

3. Relocation Tracking

Every vehicle location change is recorded, ensuring full transparency.
