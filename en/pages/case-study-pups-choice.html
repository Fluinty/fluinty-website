About Client

Pup's Choice is a brand dedicated to ensuring every dog gets the best. They offer carefully selected products for pets, focusing on pet quality and health.

Challenge

The company wanted to better understand their customers' and pets' needs to offer products perfectly tailored to breed, age, or dietary preferences. Standard newsletters weren't delivering expected results.

  • Low Effectiveness

    Mass email blasts had low open and conversion rates.

  • Lack of Data

    Lack of knowledge about specific pet needs made offer personalization impossible.

Solution

We implemented an advanced Marketing Automation system that collects data and uses it to create personalized communication.

1. Intelligent Surveys

We created interactive forms collecting detailed pet info (breed, age, allergies) in exchange for valuable tips.

2. AI Segmentation

The system automatically creates customer profiles and segments the database based on collected data, identifying groups with similar needs.

3. Personalized Campaigns

The system sends 'tailor-made' emails with product recommendations, feeding tips, and purchase reminders, perfectly matched to every pet.
