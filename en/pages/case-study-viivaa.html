About Client

Viivaa.de is an innovative recruitment platform with a mission to simplify the job search process. The company focuses on modern technologies to help candidates find the ideal job and prepare for interviews.

Challenge

Candidates often feel lost in a plethora of job offers and stressed about interviews. Manually browsing ads and lack of practice opportunities are the main barriers.

  • Time-consuming Search

    Matching CVs to hundreds of offers takes candidates too much time.

  • Pre-interview Stress

    Lack of interview simulation tools lowers candidates' chances.

Solution

We created an MVP prototype of an intelligent career assistant that supports the candidate at every stage.

1. CV Analysis (AI Parsing)

The system automatically scans the candidate's CV, extracting key skills and experience to create a digital profile.

2. Intelligent Matching

AI algorithms compare the candidate's profile with the job database, recommending only the best matches.

3. Voice Interview

An interactive AI voice module conducts simulated interviews, asking questions and providing feedback.
